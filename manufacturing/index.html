About us

Kengen Industrial Corporation has been established year 1998. It was a Manufacturing Company that manufactures quality umbrellas. It was located at Brgy. Salapungan, Gerona, Tarlac and managed by Ms. Jenalyn Tolentino.

The materials used for creating umbrellas are purchased directly from China and that is the reason why the finished products are qualified for export to other countries. Because of its quality product, Kengen Industrial Corporation became popular to the people of Tarlac and also in Pangasinan. Some of the Clients of Kengen are Politicians, Supermarkets, Banks, and some government agencies. The maximum quota of every production is 100 umbrellas. For every production the workers are ensuring that every umbrellas gone through quality control.

As of now, Kengen Industrial Corporation is looking forward for more clients as they are engaging with the modernization of all the designs for their quality products and as well as for a much affordable price.
